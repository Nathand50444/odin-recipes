British Cup of Tea

Description

Tea drinking is ingrained in the culture here in England and the entire UK. Put the items in your teacup in the wrong order or *gasp* use a microwave and you’ll be starting arguments to last years. So, I thought I’d share how to make British tea the “proper” way!

Ingredients

  • Tea bag
  • Tea cup
  • Milk
  • Sugar (optional)
  1. Boil the water.
  2. Steep the tea bag for approximately three minutes.
  3. Next, add the sugar/sweetener (optional) to the cup/s and stir well until it’s dissolved.
  4. Finally, add the milk, starting with just a splash and increasing to taste.
